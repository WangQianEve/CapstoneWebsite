About us
TEAM TRUFFLIN
As the foremost dark-pool trading platform, Liquidnet has led the way into privatized and anonymized trading, especially for institutional investors like large organizations on the scale of banks and insurance companies. Their technologies have focused on innovation and value to the trader over simple trade execution, which embodies their belief that improving the trader’s liquidity opportunities comes first.
In the scope of this project, Liquidnet aims to do the following: better understand the state of their many users while using their product, especially when dealing with time-sensitive alerts; define ways to understand their user’s behaviors and needs and translate needs into targeted content; and finally, explore the complexity of technology and data-driven design in the development of new products.
