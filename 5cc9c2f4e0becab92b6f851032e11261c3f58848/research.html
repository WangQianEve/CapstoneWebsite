Research
Our team onboarded into the financial sphere using generative research. We studied the traders' day and their work environment, including interactions with software. From this work, we understood traders' needs and desires from Liquidnet's software.
Overview
We are a group of 5 Carnegie Mellon University students studying in the Master of Human-Computer Interaction program, which is under the School of Computer Science. To find out more about us and our client, go to About page.
Problem

We need to understand the trader's daily interactions, both good and bad, with Liquidnet. Why do they use what they use?

Challenge

Our team had to understand Liquidnet 5 well enough level to design for Liquidnet’s future, without knowing every detail of it.
