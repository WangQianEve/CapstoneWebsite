Ideation
Using research as the backbone of our work, our team began imagining concepts, experiences, and software prototypes. At its core, our work centered around creating trading experiences that are positive enough to keep traders coming back to Liquidnet. We do this by redesigning interactions that show traders the 'hidden value' Liquidnet has, and how to use that to their advantage.
