Team Trufflin members are well-versed in studying human behavior with backgrounds in various fields. Like the pig that digs for the ‘black gold’ of truffles, we are digging into the financial sphere and uprooting opportunities for Liquidnet as they delve into user research and design.
Overview
In this phase, we collected information about our users and "dark-pool institutional trading". Our work focused on the trader (the user) or their technology (the product).
Problem

We need to understand the trader's daily interactions, both good and bad, with Liquidnet.

Challenge

Our team had to understand Liquidnet 5 well enough level to design for Liquidnet’s future, without knowing every detail of it.

Conceptual Journey
The team began learning about trader's lifestyles and technology. Once we had a better understanding of a trader's life, we studied Liquidnet's product. Finally, we targeted current interactions with Liquidnet 5.

Concept Testing
With a better understanding of traders' work, our team studied the Liquidnet software. We learned about the target customers and the features marketed towards them. Then, we compared the target to the actual users of Liquidnet.
Customers
An entire company or a single trader may purchase Liquidnet for their trading teams. When interacting wtih the customer, Liquidnet must keep the customer’s portfolio in mind.
Features
Liquidnet has acquired analytics technology to build upon its order management and execution software. They want their customers to do more than execute trades on Liquidnet.
Users
The actual users of Liquidnet often avoid the front-end product and features. Traders want tools to be easily learned and their value apparent, not hidden behind a stream of actions.
