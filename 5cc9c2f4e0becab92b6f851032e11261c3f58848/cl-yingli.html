Ideation
Team Trufflin members are well-versed in studying human behavior be it from a variety of fields; our backgrounds include but are not limited to business, cognitive sciences, development, and design. Like the pig that digs for the ‘black gold’ of truffles, we are digging into the financial sphere and uprooting opportunities for Liquidnet as they delve into user research and design.
Problem

We need to understand the trader's daily interactions, both good and bad, with Liquidnet. Why do they use what they use?

Challenge

Our team had to understand Liquidnet 5 well enough level to design for Liquidnet’s future, without knowing every detail of it.
